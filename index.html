Preparation Strategy

Some Points to Remeber

  • If you want to top the exam, then dont search for motivation from outside world be self motivated.
  • Success is not accidental you have to Work hard to achieve it.
  • Make a plan according to your comfort zone, dont rely on others timetable because we all are in different situation.
  • Make the list of important concepts.
  • Make a note of the important points.
  • revise all the important formula
  • Prepare cheatsheet for your revision time
  • solving previous years question papers.
  • divide the subject on the basis of its weightage and then prepare accordingly
  • clear your doubts. dont just leave it. There is always High probability that in exam you will encounter your doubts
  • dont run behind so many books, choose appropriate and prepare from there

    Time Management

    Where there is a will, there is a way. Proper time management is the key to success

    • learn to prioritize your times.
    • Think twice before doing anything, ask to yourself whether it is important to do that
    • Third list item to close it out
    • Devote your time to each subject smartly.
    • make the list of Dos and Donts
    • Study in small duration and take a break.
    • 8 hours study is enough to bag 2-3 digit rank.if you are aiming for single digit the push your limits
    • Make a Routine and study in that direction
    • make yourTime Table Light and flexible dont take too much pressure
    • Keep Modifying Your time table time tot time
    • Dont seek motivation outside. be self motivated. sometime we waste our time in watching motivated video in Youtube
